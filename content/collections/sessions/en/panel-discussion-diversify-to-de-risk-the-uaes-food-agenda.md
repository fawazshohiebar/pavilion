<p>Diversification is the UAE’s north star for food security. Under the National Food Security Strategy 2051, Abu Dhabi is building capacity across crops, aquaculture, and novel foods - from plant-based proteins to precision fermentation and alternative feed proteins. Yet, technology alone is not enough. Finance, regulation, supply chains, talent, and R&amp;D are the enablers of real resilience. This session unpacks how Abu Dhabi is weaving these strands together to lead in the Global Food Security Index.</p>
