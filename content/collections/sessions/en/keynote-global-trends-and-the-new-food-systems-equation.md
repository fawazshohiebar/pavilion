<p>The global food system is being redefined. Climate shocks, water scarcity, and population growth are rewriting the rules of supply and demand. Technology, capital, and regulation are racing to keep pace. In this keynote, we explore the megatrends reshaping the food equation, and why Abu Dhabi is emerging as a global launchpad for future food solutions.</p>
