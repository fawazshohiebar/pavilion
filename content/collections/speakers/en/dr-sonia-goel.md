Dr. Sonia Goel is a biotechnology and plant science professional with extensive experience in agricultural innovation, sustainability, and food systems research. Her work focuses on integrating metabolomics, proteomics, and microbiome technologies to enhance crop productivity, soil health, and nutritional quality.

She has collaborated with global research organizations and Agri-Tech companies Olobion Spain Labs, on advanced R&D initiatives connecting field-to-fork systems through high-end omics and biotechnology platforms. Dr. Goel has been actively involved in developing bio-based solutions for climate-resilient agriculture, supporting farmers, and promoting sustainable food security across the UAE, India, and international markets.

In addition to her scientific contributions, she has written thought-leadership articles on women in agriculture, sustainable nutrition, and technological innovations in agri-food systems. 

Dr. Goel continues to work towards fostering collaborations in system biology-driven agriculture and promoting innovative partnerships between academia, industry, and government to advance the future of sustainable food production.
