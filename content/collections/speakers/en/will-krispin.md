Will Krispin is the Director of Corporate Partnerships at The EVERY Company, a leading precision fermentation firm producing animal-free proteins for the global food industry. He oversees strategic manufacturing partnerships and government collaborations that are scaling EVERY’s production capacity across the U.S. and internationally.

Before joining EVERY, Will served as Global Chief of Staff for Business Development at Uber, managing strategic partnerships across logistics, mobility, and emerging technology. He also led technology partnership teams and M&A initiatives spanning multiple markets.

Originally from Germany, Will has lived and worked in China and now resides in California. His career centers on building partnerships that translate breakthrough technologies into scalable, real-world systems — spanning digital platforms, B2B SaaS, and biotechnology.
