Fatima Al Dhaheri is head of the Food and Water cluster at the Abu Dhabi Investment Office (ADIO).

In her role, she helps in driving Abu Dhabi's efforts in advancing research and development, sustainability and security in the food and water sector. Her work addresses shifting dietary patterns, capitalizes on technological advancements and supports the Emirate's goals of strengthening global food security, ensuring a reliable and resilient supply chain for the future.
