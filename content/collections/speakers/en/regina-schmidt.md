Regina Schmidt is the Head of SDG2 Acceleration at the United Nations World Food Programme (WFP) Innovation Accelerator. Her role is to identify, enable and scale high-impact innovations to accelerate WFP's journey to end hunger and malnutrition.

Her portfolio of ventures is addressing a wide range of humanitarian and development issues, including food system transformation, climate change, supply chain management or emergency preparedness & response.

Game-changing solutions are directly piloted in the field by developing user-centric value propositions, robust proofs of concept, and sustainable financial business models. These efforts are empowered by cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning (AI/ML), geospatial analytics or satellite data.

Regina has over 25 years of experience in innovation management, growth and business development. Prior to joining the Accelerator, Regina worked in the consumer goods sector and was Partner at a leading strategy consulting company supporting global companies in more than 30 countries and 4 continents.
